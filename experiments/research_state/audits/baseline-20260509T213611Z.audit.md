## Diagnosis

Mixed gradient accumulation fixed much of the recency-order target failure: the
model can now internalize enough edited facts to reach 0.7500 locked target
exact. The remaining weakness is locality. Replay selection still treats the
CounterFact stream as a single broad family, so anchors from sparse or
high-overlap relation groups do not reliably counterbalance same-relation answer
mode bleed.

## Next Baseline

The v6 branch keeps v5's rank-32 mixed optimization but changes replay selection:

- CounterFact replay families are stratified by public `relation_id`
- anchor replay weight is increased
- final consolidation remains mixed
- no retrieval, answer selection, visible prompt rehearsal, or locked prompt use

Expected improvement: preserve the 0.7500 target floor while increasing locked
anchor/joint success through relation-balanced specificity rehearsal.
